Your specialist for tile and natural stone imports

The latest design paired with excellent quality for a great price: this are tiles made by ENMON Heidelberg. We develop and produce tiles worldwide which are geared perfectly to our target markets regarding aesthetics, quality requirements, and trends.

Despite quickly changing trends and a growing diversity of designs in the tile industry we understand how to connect the needs of our clients with the requirements of the market. Thanks to the new branch in Sasuollo we’ve reached another milestone in the development of our business. Over the course of the last few years ENMON has made a name for itself with many European tile dealers by offering attractive tiles with a great price-performance ratio. Besides excellent products, ENMON offers a matured marketing package. Whether it is packaging, sales, presentation, or reliable logistics- ENMON knows the needs of the tile business. More than 10 million square meters of annual tile sales speak for themselves.

More than 1000 satisfied customers from Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Italy, France, Portugal, Spain, Netherlands, Belgium- mostly specialist retailers- are relying on ENMON products. We are also supplying several wholesalers for their do-it-yourself departments.
